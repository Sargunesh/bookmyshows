BookMyShow - Cookies Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to BookMyShow's Cookies Policy page. This page explains how we use cookies on our website.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help us improve your experience and provide relevant information.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improving our services and website

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.
